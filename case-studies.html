Fusion Fitness: Turning Clicks into Members

The Client: A mid-sized regional gym chain looking to scale their membership base across 12 physical locations.

The Challenge: High ad spend was failing to convert into actual memberships. Local SEO was neglected, and their social media presence was inconsistent and amateur, creating a trust barrier.

Our Solution: A Holistic Digital Overhaul

  • Local SEO: Optimized all Google Business Profiles for high local visibility, targeting "gym near me" searches.
  • Paid Ads: Reworked Facebook and Instagram campaigns from simple traffic goals to conversion-focused lead forms, using high-quality video creative (Reels Editing).
  • Social Management: Implemented professional Instagram Page Handling with custom Social Media Post Designing to elevate brand perception.
